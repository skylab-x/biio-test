Prestashop-Odoo-Bridge(POB)

Bi-Directional Export/Update of data between Prestahsop and Prestashop-Odoo-Bridge

If you want your Prestashop e-commerce store to synchronize with Odoo (formerly known as openERP) and also from Odoo to Prestashop, then Prestashop Odoo Bridge (POB) is the product suitable for your requirement.

Prestashop-Odoo-Bridge(POB) module allows you to Export/Update products, product's categories, product attributes and attribute values to Prestashop. Orders are also imported in real-time from Prestahsop to Odoo.

Easy Configuration of Module

Just fill in the Base URL of your Prestahsop Instance followed by '/api'

Then fill in the Webservices Key generated at Prestahsop.

Fill in other other details according to your choice and then test the connection.

One Click Product Export/Update to Prestashop

Export all the new Products at once or export selected products

Export all Products to Prestashop

Export all the new products (which are not already exported to Prestashop), with just one button click.

Export a single Product

You can select and export only one or seleted products to Prestahsop

Confirmation Message

Stay informed about all operations with success/failure messages on each operation.
